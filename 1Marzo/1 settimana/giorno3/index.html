Focaccia with Caramelized Onopns,
Cranberries,and Brie

Wow your taste buds whit this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sewwt caramelized onions,tart
cranberries,and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

Ingredients
    For the focaccia
  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 teaspoons istant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspoons salt
  • 13/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil,plus more for thr pan
    For the topping
  • 3 medium yellow onions,thinly sliced
  • 1 tabelspoons balsamic vinegear
  • 1-2 tabelspoons olive oil for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes(scrape rind of with a parking knife is desired,though it's not necessary)
  • 11/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries
Method
    Make the focaccia dough
  1. In a medium-sized mixing bowl,or in a stand mixer whit a paddle attachment,mix toghter
    all the dough ingredient until the flour is fully absorbed.You don't need to actually knead the
    dough,though I sometimes knead it a few times in the blow when mixing by hand to incorporate
    the last of the flour...
